App Description

The users of the application will be undergraduate students at the University of Washington, specifically for students who have not delcared a major yet. This application can also be used for students who are trying to decide what courses to take to fulfill their department requirements. In addition, academic advisers can also use this application to guide students and help them with the application processes for their interest of major.

The users of the application will be viewing information from the UW websites. Most of the information that the users will be viewing will be drawn from the UW Degree Programs website. Information from UW's MyPlan website will also be condensed into our application to help users easily find the descriptions of the courses they are interested in. The list of undergraduate majors on the UW website and their capacity will also be used as information to help develop our web application.

The web application will be a comprehensive platform for all separate department information. Users will be able to interact with the application by choosing how to filter the majors they want to explore. Additionally, users can also interact with the application by typing and searching by department or major. Users will also be able to click onto external links for more information. For example, users can click on a link for a required course, which will take the user to the MyPlan description of the course. Our app will help solve the problems of students feeling overwhelmed, lost and confused, and therefore help students succeed academically. By providing a user-friendly app, students will be able to navigate and explore their major of interest with ease. Our app will allow users to prepare and start early in academic planning, providing a seamless experience for those who are struggling.
